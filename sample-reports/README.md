# Sample Medical Reports for Multi-Clinic System

This directory contains sample medical reports that can be used for testing and demonstration purposes of the chatbot functionality.

## Available Sample Reports

1. **Type 2 Diabetes Management Report** - Comprehensive report for diabetes patient management
2. **Hypertension Follow-up Report** - Blood pressure monitoring and medication adjustment
3. **Annual Physical Examination Report** - Routine health checkup findings
4. **Respiratory Infection Treatment Report** - Acute illness diagnosis and treatment plan

## Usage

These reports contain realistic medical information that can be:
- Uploaded to the system by doctors after appointments
- Queried by the chatbot to answer patient questions
- Used to demonstrate the chatbot's ability to extract and summarize medical information

All patient information in these samples is fictional and for demonstration purposes only.
